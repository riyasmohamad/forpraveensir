site to see

some important places we will see on the trip

bolgatty palace

On Bolgatty island in Kochi, Kerala, the Dutch once constructed Bolgatty Palace. The palace was constructed in 1744, making it one of the oldest Dutch palaces outside of Holland. Bolgatty Palace is currently a hotel managed by KTDC. Additionally, wedding ceremonies are conducted.

vallarpadam container terminal

The first transshipment facility in India and the first container terminal to operate in a Special Economic Zone is the Kochi international container trasshipment terminal, also known locally as the vallarpadam terminal. It is a piece of kochi's Cochin Port.

oil tanker birth

An oil tanker, sometimes referred to as a petroleum tanker, is a ship created specifically for the bulk transportation of oil or its byproduct.Crude tankers and product tankers are the two basic types of oil tankers.Size and profession are two factors that are frequently used to categorise oil tankers.

dolphin areas

The Arabian Sea, Periyar, and Vembanattu Kayal converge at Marine Drive.There is a good probability that you may observe dolphins in the vicinity of the rivers Vembanatt Kayal and Pariyar, one of which flows from the north and the other from the south.If you're lucky, dolphins will be in the area.

chinese fishing net

In India and Indonesia, Chinese fishing nets are a sort of stationary lift net. They are permanent land-based fishing installations called fishing nets. Although they are more formally termed as "shore operated lift nets," these nets are also known as "Chinese fishing nets" in India.

cochin shipyard

An Indian shipbuilding plant is called Cochin Shipyard Ltd. It is one of a number of maritime-related facilities in Kerala, India's port city of Kochi. The shipyard offers a variety of services, including the construction of double-hulled oil tankers and platform supply vessels.
